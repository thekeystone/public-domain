Plagiarism Masker

This web app attempts to mask any plagiarism attempts in your paper. This app works by automatically replacing Latin characters with identical Cyrillic ones. No guarentees are made by this app. Plagiarism may still be detected, depending on the software used. Plagiarism is a serious problem in the academic world and the sole intention of this app is a proof-of-concept. Please do not use actually attempt to use this app in an essay or other academic paper.
